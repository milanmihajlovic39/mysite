Our brains behave like a beachball filled with bees. Hundreds of conflicting impulses, pushing us in different directions.
People never want to do one thing. We want to do all the things. We simultaneously want to exercise and to learn Spanish and to go out for pizza.
Our desires are countless, independent agents, working to nudge our beachball in their own selfish direction.
And so usually, that ball is going nowhere. It’s controlled more by the terrain than by the will of what’s inside it.

So I'm going to focus on one thing at a time while following this list:

  • Be able to design a site or edit a pre-existing site's design
  • Build site concepts based on what users want and need
  • Craft a mobile version of a site
  • Have excellent, accessible, semantic, lightweight, yummy, master-level, cutting edge and beyond HTML/CSS skills
  • Bring the AJAX and jQuery
  • Collect and analyze analytics
  • Run an A/B test
  • Run an ad-hoc user testing session
  • Wrangle at least one CMS, preferably more
